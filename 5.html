Nikola Tesla

The Man Who Invented the Twentieth Century

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."

Key Contributions

  • Alternating Current (AC): Developed the modern AC electricity supply system.
  • Tesla Coil: Invented in 1891, used in radio technology and medical devices.
  • Induction Motor: Revolutionized industrial manufacturing.
  • Wireless Telegraphy: Early pioneer of radio waves and remote control.

His Legacy

Tesla was a visionary whose ideas were often far ahead of his time. Today, his name is synonymous with innovation, and his work continues to influence modern power distribution and wireless communication.
